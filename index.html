Brand Calling

The Story

Until now, the boundaries of paint have kept true color from shining through. Devine Color, a boutique paint brand for those who see color as more than a number on a swatch, aimed to change that by developing a formula that allows the color you imagine to show up on your walls in its purest form. New Honor Society developed the brand positioning, style guide and marketing plan for the launch.
