The Story
Until now, the boundaries of paint have kept true color from shining through. Devine Color, a boutique paint brand for those who see color as more than a number on a swatch, aimed to change that by developing a formula that allows the color you imagine to show up on your walls in its purest form. New Honor Society developed the brand positioning, style guide and marketing plan for the launch.

Heidi & Holly

Heidi Singleton and Holly Aguirre (aka “HiHo”) have partnered for over 15 years, forming an uncommonly collaborative approach to creative and account service relationships, agency life and client work. As Co-Managing Directors, Holly brings strong business acumen, strategic thinking and client focused leadership while Heidi is the creative visionary, ensuring that the work and agency culture lives into the mantra of Less Sell, More Soul.

The foundation for their success is the shared view that great brands aren’t just good for business, they are good for the world and the people they serve. And to help fulfill on that vision, creatives are encouraged to explore multiple outlets for innovative thinking, accountexecs are cultivators of both creative work and client partnerships, and everyone is collectively working toward more meaningful marketing for every brand.
